Cycling Infrastructure Prioritisation Toolkit (CyIPT)

A DfT-funded research project at the University of Leeds

Welcome to CyIPT, a research project funded by the UK Department for Transport.

CyIPT will be a tool aiming to provide an evidence-base for prioritisation of transport infrastructure that will get more people cycling.

More information on the project will be available here shortly.

You have reached the development area, which is not publicly accessible.

You can contact us by e-mail via the details on the Contacts page linked above.

Note: CyIPT is a tool to aid decision-making, not to make the decisions for you.
